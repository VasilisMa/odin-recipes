Grliled Salmon Tacos

Description

Igredients

Marinated Salmon

  • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 1/3 cup chopped cliantro
  • 1/3 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 garlic clove, pressed
  • 2 pounds skinless salmon flilets, cut into 1-inch cubes

Tacos

  • 4 skewers
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 1/2 red onion, cut into wedges
  • 10 (8 inch) flour tortillas, warmed
  • 1 1/2 cups prepared coleslaw
  • 1/2 mini seedless cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 2 radishes, thinly sliced
  • 1 green onion, thinly sliced (optional)

Directions

  1. Mix light brown sugar, white pepper, cilantro, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and garlic together in a bowl. Add salmon and marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Beginning and ending with salmon, thread salmon, green pepper, red pepper, and red onions onto 4 skewers.
  4. Cook salmon skewers on the hot grill until fish is opaque in color, turning every 5 minutes, about 15 minutes total.
  5. To each tortilla add coleslaw, cucumbers, and radishes and top with salmon. Garnish with sliced green onions.
